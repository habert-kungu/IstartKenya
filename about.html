About Us

iSTART Kenya is a pioneering educational institution dedicated to empowering Kenyan youth through innovative STEM education and global exposure. Our mission is to bridge the technology gap and create opportunities for the next generation of African innovators.

Founded in 2020, we have successfully trained over 5,000 students in various technology programs and facilitated life-changing educational trips to Silicon Valley for more than 200 students.
